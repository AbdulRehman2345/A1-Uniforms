About Us

Our Company

A-1 UNIFORMS is a leading provider of school uniforms. The company has been supplying schools since 2016 and currently provides uniforms for primary and secondary schools. We work with local embroiderers that provide some of the best embroidery services that there is; so you can have full peace of mind that your school logos will be correctly displayed on your uniform and last through all of the stresses that school uniforms have to endure.

Our Vision

Create a Holistic Supply side functioning for Uniforms and become Customer’s leading choice.

Our Mission

we are dedicated to cater the Uniform needs across the country. We aim to redefine the traditional monotony, offering the elements of quality, comfort and convenience.

Our Core Values

Our Core Values are Quality, Comfort and Convenience. Our Uniforms are stitched to durability and are comfortable to wear. We believe in delivering the absolute best wear for your daily use, that lasts long and is reliable enough for you to trust every time you purchase from us.

Our Product Quality

What makes a school uniform a Trutex uniform? We put all our expertise and dedication into making our garments work for you whilst maintaining their style and long durability. We invest in product innovation that helps uniform stay looking like new for longer.

Why A-1 Uniforms

High-quality fabric is used in all our products to ensure you have a comfortable experience and you’re safe from any color bleeding incidents. Standardized cloth material is procured to maintain quality, ensure no color variation and provide standard prices.

Uniforms are produced with durable and strong stitching practices, on slim fit patterns and instilling the use of secured threads such that no irritations are experienced.All accessories used in producing your uniforms, including elastic, buttons, hooks, zippers, care labels, hangtags etc. are Oeko-tex certified, which means our products are harmless in terms of human ecological environment.

We use stainless steel hooks and high-quality nylon/brass zippers in pants, shorts, skirts etc. These accessories provide the advantage of being corrosion resistant and durable.
